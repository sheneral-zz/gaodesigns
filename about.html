Hi, I'm Shen.

Emerging professional in graphic design and front-end web development, I am a self-professed generalist with a wide range of interests. I have a sharp eye for typography, and enjoy tackling design problems especially in the field of UX/UI design—something I have an intuitive understanding of.

One of my many interests is type design. I have always been fascinated with the different "feelings" typefaces can convey, ever since I was a toddler living in China. I am constantly mesmerized by fonts I come across, whether they be Chinese- or Latin-alphabet-based.

Currently, I am living in the San Francisco Bay Area and open to opportunities to collaborate! Feel free to say hi!
